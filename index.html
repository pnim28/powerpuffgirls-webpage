Powerpuff Girls

Blossom, the "Everything nice" part of the trio, is the Powerpuff Girls' confident and courageous leader. Dubbed "Commander and the Leader," she is best known for her level head and determination, as well as leading the girls to victory and saving the day. She first appeared alongside Bubbles and Buttercup in the Whoopass Stew short "A Sticky Situation!". She is the overall main protagonist of the series.
